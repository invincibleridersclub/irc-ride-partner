IRC Ride Partner – Data Deletion Request

This page allows users of the IRC Ride Partner app, developed by Invincible Riders Club, to request deletion of their account and data.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Click the button below to open the Data Deletion Form.
  2. Fill in your registered email or phone number.
  3. Select the type of data you want to delete (e.g., entire account, ride history, etc.).
  4. Submit the form. Our team will verify your identity and process the request.
Request Data Deletion
What happens after deletion:
  • Your account and associated data will be deleted within 7 business days.
  • Data types deleted: Authentication info, ride history, expenses, to-do lists.
  • We may retain crash logs or analytics for diagnostics (not linked to your identity).

For questions or issues, contact us at club.invincibleriders@gmail.com.
